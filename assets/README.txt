# Birthday Surprise - Photo & Music Guide 💖

I've set up this folder so you can easily add your own personal photos and music to the surprise!

### 📸 How to add your Photos:
1. Find your favorite photos on your computer.
2. Move/Copy them into the `assets/images/` folder.
3. **Rename** them exactly like this:
   - `heart-photo.jpg` -> This will be the main 3D flipping heart in the center.
   - `memory1.jpg` -> The first Polaroid in the gallery.
   - `memory2.jpg` -> The second Polaroid.
   - `memory3.jpg` -> The third Polaroid.

### 🎵 How to add Background Music:
1. Find an MP3 file you want to play (e.g., a romantic song).
2. Move it into this `assets/` folder.
3. Rename it to `romantic.mp3`.

Once you've added and renamed the files, just refresh `index.html` in your browser and they will appear beautifully!
